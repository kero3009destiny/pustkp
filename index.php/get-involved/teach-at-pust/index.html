Teach / Work at PUST

Pyongyang University of Science and Technology welcomes qualified educators in the fields of study to volunteer their time to teach at the university. Those seeking to teach at PUST should share in the vision of the university and support the general ethos with which the university was founded. A Master’s, PhD, or language certification is required in the field of study that applicants desire to teach.

Please note that all positions are on a volunteer basis and are not paid positions.

We are currently looking to fill these positions.

If you feel that you have the required qualifications to join the foreign faculty at PUST and would like to learn more about teaching at PUST, please email us at hr@pust.co.
